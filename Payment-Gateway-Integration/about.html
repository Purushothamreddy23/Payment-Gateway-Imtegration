About Us

We connect students of all financial backgrounds with experts and promote knowledge sharing to enable equal opportunity for all. Sparks Donation Hub is a non-political, non-profit organisation committed to the development of rural India. Donation began in a remote village in the Chhattisgarh District. It was registered under The Societies Registration Act of 1860. This hub is now working in over 100 villages.
